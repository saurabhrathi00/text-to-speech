About SastaSpeech

Last updated: {{ updated_at }}

SastaSpeech is an expressive AI text-to-speech service built for creators worldwide — YouTubers, podcasters, educators, and storytellers who need natural-sounding voiceovers without a recording studio.

What we do

You type a script in almost any language. We refine it for natural pronunciation (using Google Gemini) and synthesize studio-quality audio (using ElevenLabs v3). Output is ready to drop into your video editor in seconds.

Why we built this

Most studio-quality TTS tools are expensive and lock real emotional range behind premium tiers. SastaSpeech costs from ₹49 to ₹1999/month — a fraction of a dubbing studio — and gives every plan per-sentence emotion control and natural, multilingual narration.
