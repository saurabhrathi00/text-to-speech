About SastaSpeech

Last updated: {{ updated_at }}

SastaSpeech is a Hindi text-to-speech service built for Indian creators — YouTubers, podcasters, educators, and storytellers who need natural-sounding voiceovers without a recording studio.

What we do

You type a Hindi, Hinglish, or English script. We refine it for natural pronunciation (using Google Gemini) and synthesize studio-quality audio (using ElevenLabs). Output is ready to drop into your video editor in seconds.

Why we built this

Existing TTS tools are expensive, English-first, and don't handle Hinglish the way Indian audiences actually speak. SastaSpeech costs from ₹49 to ₹1999/month — a fraction of dubbing studios — and is tuned for natural Hindi narration with per-sentence emotion control.
