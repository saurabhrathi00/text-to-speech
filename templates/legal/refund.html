Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: {{ updated_at }}

Digital service — instant delivery

SastaSpeech is a software-as-a-service product. Audio is generated and delivered the moment you click "Generate", consuming third-party costs (ElevenLabs, Gemini API) immediately. Because of this, refunds are not available for already-used credits.

Eligible for refund

You may request a full refund if ALL of these are true:

  • You have NOT used any credits / generations from the plan.
  • The request is made within {{ biz.refund_window_days }} days of payment.
  • The plan was purchased through our payment processor (proof of transaction available).

Partial refund cases

Service-side outages causing extended unavailability (>24 hrs) qualify for pro-rata credit toward your next billing cycle. Email {{ biz.support_email }} with details.

Not eligible

  • Already-consumed generations or characters.
  • Day passes / top-ups after any generation has been made.
  • Accounts suspended for violation of Terms.

How to request

Email {{ biz.support_email }} with:

  1. Registered email address.
  2. Transaction ID (from your payment processor).
  3. Reason for refund.

We respond within {{ biz.support_response_sla|pl }} on {{ biz.support_hours }}. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method within {{ biz.refund_processing|pl }}.

Cancellation

Subscriptions are non-renewing by default — they simply expire after the stated validity. There's no cancellation step needed.
