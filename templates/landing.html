Frequently asked questions

What is SastaSpeech?

An expressive AI text-to-speech platform for creators worldwide. Type a script in almost any language and get studio-quality narrator audio in seconds.

Is there a free plan?

Yes — one generation per day up to 100 characters. Enough to try the full pipeline before paying.

Which languages does it support?

Dozens — English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, Portuguese, Arabic, and many more. Voices use ElevenLabs v3 with native-sounding prosody in each language.

How do emotion tags work?

Toggle the Emotion-tags option and Gemini classifies every sentence, picking the right inline tag — [happy], [sobbing], [whispers], [giggles], [breathless], and 50+ more. The TTS model then performs the cue.

How long does each audio take?

Under 10 seconds for a typical 500-char script. Text refinement + voice synthesis happen in parallel.

What's the quick top-up?

A one-time ₹49 add-on: 1,500 extra characters, 3 bonus generations, 500-char per-request limit. Stacks on whatever plan you're already on.

Can I use the audio commercially?

Yes. On any paid plan, audio is yours to use in YouTube videos, podcasts, ads, courses, or commercial work.

How do I upgrade?

Sign in → click Upgrade → pick a plan → pay via the contact channel shown. Admin approves within minutes.
